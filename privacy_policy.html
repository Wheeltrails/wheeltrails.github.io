Wheeltrails - Privacy Policy

Posted: July 05, 2022

Thanks for using Wheeltrails. Here we describe our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when You use the Wheeltrails app.

Wheeltrails is an application created and developed by Joseph Wilk

Collection of Personal Data

The Wheeltrails app available from the Google Play store or the Apple App Store does not collect any personally identifiable information.

All data created through the app is stored on your device only, and can be simply erased by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it.

Access to your Media Library

The Wheeltrails App requests access to your media library to support downloading of your Wheeltrail photos to your device. None of your media library content is read or used in anyway by the Wheeltrails App. All photos taken through the Wheeltrails App are only stored on your device.

Access to your Camera

The Wheeltrails App is an augmented reality application which overlays your camera's live view with digital layers (in our case trails). This is the only use of the camera the Wheeltrails App uses.
